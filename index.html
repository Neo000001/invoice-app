What Is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a sales invoice that contains required details like supplier name, GSTIN, invoice number, invoice date, taxable value, tax breakdown and amount payable. It is essential for GST-registered businesses in India to issue a GST invoice when supplying goods or services to their customers.
