Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Welcome to Eebii Free GST Invoice Generator. At Eebii, we take your privacy and personal data seriously. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it gets used, our stringent data protection commitments, and the choices you hold regarding your personal information when using our web services.

1. Information Collection and Local Processing

The Free Invoice Generator (No Storage)

Our foundational principle for the Free GST Invoice Generator is absolute data minimization. We engineered the free tool to explicitly perform all heavy lifting right within your local browser's memory cache (client-side processing).

This means when you type in sensitive data like your Company Name, GSTIN, Client details, or transaction amounts, we do not upload, store, or intercept this invoice data through our servers. You are not required to create an account, register an email, or log in. Because your invoice data never hits a backend database, it is not vulnerable to remote server breaches. When you download the finished PDF, the temporary browser session handles the document rendering locally.

The Eebii Pro SaaS Platform

For users who intentionally sign up for our premium Pro SaaS Platform, we do collect necessary identifiers including your name, verified business email address, billing information, and historical invoice data. This enables us to provide the persistent cloud features you pay for, such as saving previous clients, tracking paid versus unpaid invoices over time, and offering multi-device synchronization. This data is rigorously encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

2. Automatically Collected Information and Analytics

Like most standard web applications, to maintain system performance, security, and understand our user traffic, our servers may automatically log certain baseline connection data. This can include your generic IP address, browser type (Chrome, Safari, etc.), operating system, and the timestamps of your visiting sessions. This data is strictly aggregated to troubleshoot crashes or monitor load balancing.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising (Google AdSense)

To sustainably keep our Free Invoice Generator 100% free with no annoying paywalls, we partner with third-party advertising networks, specifically Google AdSense, to display non-intrusive advertisements on our site.

These third-party vendors use tracking technologies like cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites across the internet. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads correlated to your browsing interests. You possess full control to opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or utilizing browser extensions that strictly limit cookie tracking.

4. Our Commitment to Data Security

We implement robust physical, technical, and administrative security measures designed to safeguard any information you provide us against accidental, unlawful, or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, access, or disclosure. As mentioned above, since the vast majority of our free traffic never actually sends data to an external server block, technical exposure is drastically minimized by default architecture.

5. Changes to This Privacy Statement

As internet regulations and our service architectures evolve, we reserve the right to continually amend and refine this Privacy Policy. When structural changes occur, we will proactively update the precise "Effective Date" logged at the very top of this page. We strongly encourage you to periodically review this document so you systematically understand how Eebii protects your data over time.

6. Contact the Eebii Privacy Team

Your trust is non-negotiable for us. If you have any questions, pressing concerns, or formal suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy or our overall data retention practices, please do not hesitate to reach out. You can communicate with us via our Contact page or by emailing our data protection officer directly at support@eebii.com.
